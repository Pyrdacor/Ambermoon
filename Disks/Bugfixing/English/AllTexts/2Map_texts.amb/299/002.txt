AS YOU ENTER THIS PART OF THE CAVE A BLINDING LIGHT SUDDENLY APPEARS FROM WHICH A MAN IN A DARK ROBE STEPS FORWARD. HE SAYS:^ "IF THERE IS ONE THING I CANNOT BEAR, IT IS UNWANTED VISITORS - OR PERHAPS LEBAB SENT YOU HERE?^ WHATEVER, YOUR LIFE WILL CERTAINLY END HERE NOW."^ WITH THESE WORDS HE CLAPS HIS HANDS AND SEVERAL GARGOYLES APPEAR.
