THERE ARE MANY EFFECTIVE HERBS ON LYRAMION. THESE ARE THE THREE MOST IMPORTANT ONES:^ - THE SWAMP LILY: THIS ONLY GROWS IN A SMALL SWAMP TO THE NORTH EAST OF THE TOWER OF THE ALCHEMISTS. IN THE PAST IT HAS PROVED HELPFUL AGAINST EVERY KNOWN TYPE OF POISON.^ - THE KALMIR HERB: THIS HERB GROWS ON AN ISLAND IN THE ARTIFICIAL LAKE TO THE SOUTH OF THE TOWER OF LEBAB. IT IS GENERALLY ACKNOWLEDGED THAT IT IS GOOD AT HEALING DISEASE.^ - THE FIRE THISTLE: THIS STRANGE PLANT ONLY GROWS ON A NUMBER OF GRASS-COVERED HILLS AT THE NORTHERN END OF MERA'S ISLAND. IT HAS BEEN KNOWN TO HEAL BLINDNESS.
