"I HARDLY DARED HOPE THAT HELP WOULD COME. THE FIRE DEMON LUMINOR IS HOLDING ALL THE INHABITANTS OF BURNVILLE AS PRISONERS HERE IN THE TORTURE CHAMBER. PERHAPS WE SHOULD GET TOGETHER, FOR TOGETHER WE WILL BE BETTER ABLE TO FREE THE PEOPLE.^ I SAW THAT LUMINOR PERSONALLY HOLDS THE KEY TO THE CELLS. AS FAR AS I KNOW, HE LIVES ON ONE OF THE UPPER FLOORS IN THE TOWER."
