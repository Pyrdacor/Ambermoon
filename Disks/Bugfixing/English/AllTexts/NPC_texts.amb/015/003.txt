"AS I HEAR, MY WIFE TOLD YOU HER VERSION OF THE THEFT. SHE BELIEVES FIRMLY THAT THE FIRST WINE GOBLET WAS STOLEN BY A KOBOLD OR SOMETHING SIMILAR, BUT THAT IS RUBBISH! I SURPRISED THE THIEVES DURING THEIR SECOND BURGLARY AND THEY WERE NOTHING LIKE KOBOLDS. AT LEAST THE ONE I SAW. THE OTHER CAME FROM BEHIND ME, OF COURSE."
