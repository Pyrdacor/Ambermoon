WITH A LOOK OF PRIDE THE BARON TAKES HIS CHAIN OF OFFICE AND PUTS IT ON.^ AS YOU TELL HIM THE STORY OF THE SYLPHS, HE LISTENS IN ASTONISHMENT. THEN HE SAYS:^ "I MUST GO TO MY WIFE IMMEDIATELY AND APOLOGISE TO HER! HOW COULD I MAINTAIN SHE HAD ONLY DREAMED ALL THIS? BUT BEFORE I GO, I WOULD LIKE TO PRESENT YOU WITH THIS PEARL AS A GIFT. I RECEIVED IT FROM A FISHERMAN MANY YEARS AGO. HE SAID IT WAS ONE OF THE FEW WIND PEARLS. I HOPE YOU CAN USE IT BETTER THAN I HAVE."
