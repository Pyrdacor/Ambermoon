"ALMOST FIFTEEN YEARS AGO, WHEN IT SEEMED FAIRLY CERTAIN WE COULD SURVIVE, WE BEGAN TO EXAMINE THE UNDERGROUND PARTS OF THE MOON. THE RESULTS WERE DISAPPOINTING: NO METALS, JUST A FEW MINERALS, SOME PRETTY-COLOURED ROCK FORMATIONS WHICH YOU CAN NOW SEE AS THE FLOORING OF THE THRONE ROOM, AND OFTEN A FEW LUMPS OF USELESS AMBER. BUT THE MINE IS CLOSED AT THE MOMENT."
